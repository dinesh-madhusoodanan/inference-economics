Storage · the layer under the meter

Compute gets the headline. Storage gets the recurring bill.

Inference pricing tracks the per-token cost of thinking. But every served model also sits on top of bytes that cost money every month — the weights at rest, the retrieval index behind RAG, and the logs that grow with every request. Here's where those dollars sit, and a quick estimate for your own footprint.

Model storage

Storing the brain

The weights themselves are cheap to keep, but you pay for every copy — one per region, plus warm load caches and rollback versions.

  • Weights at rest 70B · fp16 ≈ 140 GB~$2.94 / mo
  • Replication ×3 regions~$8.82 / mo
  • Version & rollback cache previous checkpoints~$4–10 / mo
  • KV cache ephemeral · billed as computein GPU mem

Data storage

Storing the context
